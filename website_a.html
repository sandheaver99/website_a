News and Press

16th February 2017

Dark Water is released on itch.io. You get get it here

Dark Water is a simple love letter to the gravity-based lander genre and draws inspiration from a number of lander arcade and PC games.

Use your [arrow-key] controls to explore the underwater caverns, destroy the flashing enemy base, rescue engineers and return to orbit. Always keep an eye on your power reserves and hull integrity!

Use the [ctrl] key to fire light pulses that will disable enemy guns and bases and help you to navigate through the dark waters.

Earn Kudos credits from your exploits and trade these to purchase upgrades for your engineers to install on your ship!
